About Me

Hi, my name is Matthew Powell, you may have noticed me posing at the top of the page! I'm currently working as a print production manager for a company which provides technology based distribution solutions for the medical and telecommunications industry. I'm looking for a career change as the longer term job security isn't great. I recently became a first time dad and enjoy spending time with my daughter. I like to keep fit, and I am a keen swimmer and footballer. I recently met a previous Founders and Coders graduate who put me in touch.

Why I Want To Become A Web Developer

I have always been interested in technology and have enjoyed working with computers since a young age. I've spent a huge amount of time over last few years learning as much about JavaScript and associated technologies as possible. I’m now so obsessed, I find myself working on my projects or learning something new every day. I genuinely enjoy the challenge of learning new technologies and get a buzz when solving problems or completing new features in my projects.

Founders and Coders

I think the Founders and Coders programme provides an amazing opportunity that will expand my knowledge and help me land a professional role. I have considered doing a paid bootcamp course, but this is something that is out of my price range. My friend which graduated through your programme a few years ago now works for Amazon, I don’t think you can get a bigger accolade than that! I have really enjoyed doing the projects. They have given me a chance to focus on some of the front-end design ideas that I haven’t paid enough attention to in the past, and given me a chance to take a deeper dive into JavaScript, covering some syntax and concepts I haven’t seen before.
